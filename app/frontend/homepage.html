HDBWise
Data-driven real estate company

Founded in 2023, HDBWise is a real estate technology company, dedicated to revolutionising the resale HDB property market in Singapore. It provides a comprehensive platform that empowers HDB buyers with seamless access to a wealth of vital data, from price trends to nearby amenities, all within a single, user-friendly software. Moreover, this software provides interactive visualisations and a dashboard that allows buyers to have a comprehensive view of the properties and thereby make an informative decision in their purchase. Read more…

The mission at HDBWise is to provide HDB buyers with a robust and all-inclusive tool that eliminates the need for traditional property agents. The mission aims to address challenges faced by buyers in the current market, including the time and effort required to find a qualified agent, the high cost associated with agent commissions, and the lack of access to all relevant data necessary to make an informed decision. HDBWise aims to simplify this process, placing the power directly in the hands of our clients.

The market for HDB resale flats has demonstrated a consistent upward trend, with an average quarter-to-quarter increase of 1.74% between 2022 Q3 and 2023 Q3, as reported by HDB (2023). Additionally, the total number of applicants registered between 2015 and 2022 has shown steady growth, averaging 5.8% annually, despite a slight decrease in the year 2022-2023 (HDB, 2023).

Hence to better cater to the eloving needs of HDB buyers and enhance their overall experience, HDBWise incorporates such features:

  • Data Access: It provides comprehensive and up-to-date data, including price trends and information about nearby amenities. This ensures that our clients have all the information they need at their fingertips.
  • Agent-Free: With HDBWise, clients can navigate the property market without needing a real estate agent. It empowers buyers to take control of their property search journey.
  • Interactive Visualisation: The application boasts interactive charts and visualisations, enabling clients to effortlessly compare different properties. This visual richness enhances decision-making and property evaluation.
